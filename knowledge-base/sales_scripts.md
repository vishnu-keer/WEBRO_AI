# WEBRO — Sales Scripts & Messaging

> Voice + patterns the Outreach/Email agents imitate. Trust-first, human, non-spammy.

## Cold email — first touch
- Hook: a specific, real observation about their site (not generic flattery).
- Value: one concrete improvement + the outcome it drives.
- Ask: low-friction (a quick reply or a 15-min look), never a hard pitch.

## Instagram / WhatsApp DM
- Short, casual, one idea, one question.

## Objection handling
- "Too expensive" → <TODO: reframe on ROI / payment options>

## Do / Don't
- Do: reference their actual business + a real finding.
- Don't: use hype, false urgency, or mass-blast language.
